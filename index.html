About Eldeluc Tech Rescue

Welcome to Eldeluc Tech Rescue, where we are passionate about technology and dedicated to providing top-notch computer repair services. Our mission is to solve your tech problems efficiently and affordably. We feature a transparent pricing structure that breaks down costs explicitly into parts, active manual bench labor, and lower-rate automated passive processing labor.

Services Offered

Hardware Repair & Upgrades

From broken screens to failing hard drives, we diagnose and repair all types of hardware issues. We also offer performance-boosting upgrades like RAM and SSD installation.

Virus & Malware Removal

Is your computer slow or behaving strangely? We'll deep-scan and remove viruses, spyware, and other malicious software to restore your system's performance and security.

Data Recovery

Accidentally deleted important files or have a failing hard drive? We offer professional data recovery services to help you retrieve your precious photos, documents, and other files.
